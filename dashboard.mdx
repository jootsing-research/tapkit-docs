The [TapKit dashboard](https://tapkit.ai/dashboard) is where you manage your account, phones, API keys, and billing. Most day-to-day usage happens in the Mac app — the dashboard is for account management.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of dashboard overview */}

## Phones

View all phones registered to your account. Activate or deactivate phones, and add new ones to your plan.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of phones list */}

## API keys

Create, view, and revoke API keys. Each key grants access to all phones on your account. See [Security & Privacy](/security) for best practices.

## Sessions

View your session history — when sessions ran, how long they lasted, and which phone was used. Your plan includes a set amount of session time per month, with overages billed separately. Monitor your usage here.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of sessions view */}

## Team

Add team members, manage roles, and configure organization settings. Team members share access to phones and session history.

## Billing

Manage your subscription, add or remove phones from your plan, and view invoices and payment history.
