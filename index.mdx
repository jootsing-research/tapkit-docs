## Overview

TapKit lets you control real iPhones programmatically through a simple API. Build AI agents, automate workflows, and test mobile apps — all on actual hardware.

- **Real iPhone control**: Execute touch gestures, launch apps, type text, and capture screenshots on physical devices — not simulators.
- **Simple API**: Run a task in just a few lines of code, making it easy to integrate into any workflow.
- **AI-native**: Built for AI agents with MCP support, OpenAI CUA integration, and vision-based control out of the box.
- **Real-time streaming**: View live phone screens via WebRTC for monitoring and debugging.
- **Python SDK**: High-level client library with intuitive methods for every phone interaction.
- **Persistent sessions**: Maintain state across multiple tasks to preserve cookies and authentication.
